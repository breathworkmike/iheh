# Breathing Practice Tool

A free, browser-based tool to help you slow down, tune in, and down-regulate your breath. Designed to guide you through calming breathwork patterns that activate the parasympathetic nervous system — reducing stress and promoting a sense of calm.

Built with care by **Mike** — a professional breath trainer and coach passionate about making breathwork accessible to everyone.

---

## What it does

This tool guides you through a structured breathing practice designed to:

- Slow your breathing rate
- Extend your exhale relative to your inhale
- Activate your body's natural relaxation response
- Build a sustainable breathwork habit over time

No app download required. No account needed. Just open and breathe.

---

## How to use it

1. Open the tool in any web browser
2. Follow the on-screen breathing guide
3. Practise for as little as 5 minutes to feel the benefit
4. Return daily for best results
